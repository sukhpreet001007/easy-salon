Appointment

Appointment Management enables 24/7 bookings via WhatsApp, online, or in-store. Real-time sync, automated reminders, and smart staff allocation reduce no-shows, improve satisfaction, and streamline operations, while integrations with billing and CRM boost efficiency and revenue.
